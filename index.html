To Piim

My dearest Piim,

From the day I walked past you and said something about your hair, to today... it's been 5 years already. I still remember the feeling of you reaching over to fix my collar on the way to work that morning — such a small thing, but I've never forgotten it.

Thank you for always taking such good care of me. Thank you for the energy and warmth you bring every single time we're together. Thank you for every workshop, every trip, every little moment we've shared.

There were times it got a little tense between us, but we always made it through. Having you by my side makes everything feel better.

I hope we keep walking this path together — through year 6, year 7, year 8, and far beyond. No stopping, no end to the counting.

With love,
Nine ♡
